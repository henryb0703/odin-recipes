Chicken Pot Pie

Description

A delicious chicken pie made from scratch with carrots, peas and celery.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Chicken
  • 1 Cup Carrots
  • 1 Cup Peas
  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 2 Cup Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Arrange pie crust in tin
  2. Put Ingredients in pie crust
  3. Cook at 500 for 2 hours
  4. Enjoy
